🏢 Enterprise Overview

NutriSmart is a digital enterprise solution designed to address real-world nutritional challenges faced by students and young people in modern society.

The system integrates data analytics, decision-support tools, and interactive user interfaces to encourage healthier dietary behaviour through accessible technology.

🌍 Real-World Problem

Poor nutrition is a growing global issue, particularly among adolescents. Increased consumption of fast food, processed snacks, and sugary drinks has led to rising rates of obesity, diabetes risk, and reduced concentration levels.

Many students lack the knowledge or tools to evaluate the nutritional quality of their food choices in real time.

🎯 Purpose of This System

The purpose of NutriSmart is to:

• Provide real-time food analysis using simple metrics (calories, sugar, fat)
• Encourage healthier food substitutions
• Improve awareness of balanced dietary habits
• Support long-term wellbeing through behavioural change

🌱 Alignment with Sustainable Development Goals

This project directly aligns with:

SDG 3: Good Health and Wellbeing

By promoting healthier food choices and reducing the consumption of high-risk foods, NutriSmart contributes to improved physical health outcomes and preventive healthcare awareness.

💼 Enterprise Value

NutriSmart demonstrates how data-driven systems can be applied in a business and educational context to solve real-world problems.

It simulates a lightweight health-tech platform that could be scaled into schools, canteens, or mobile applications.

The system also reflects key enterprise computing principles such as:

• Data collection and analysis
• User-centered design
• Decision support systems
• Digital transformation of health education

📈 Expected Impact

If implemented in real-world settings, NutriSmart could:

• Improve student dietary awareness
• Reduce unhealthy eating patterns
• Support school wellbeing programs
• Encourage long-term healthy lifestyle habits
